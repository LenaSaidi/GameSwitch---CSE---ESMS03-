CLUB SCIENTIFIQUE DE L'ESI

CSE -Club Scientifique de
L'ESI- is a student club at
the Higher National School
of Computer Sciense,
Algiers

Established in 2008 and
centred around tech and
anything related to it, It
has spread and become
recongnized nationalwide due
to it's various fruitful
events such as HACK-it and
DESIGN FEST and numerous
others.

It's an event where game design enthusiasts and
storytellers alike come together for a fun and
challenging time.

Participants will be creating a game
story/narrative and modeling aspects of the
game using the famous software 'Blender'.

While also creating promotional material in forms of
posters using their software of choice.

Seasoned veteran, passionate beginner or
anything in between.
GameSwitch will be your gateway to express
your passion and creativity.
So hope into the train and let us experience
this together!
